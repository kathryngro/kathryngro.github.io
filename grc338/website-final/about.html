About us

Kathryn's Art Gallery was founded in 2003 by Kathryn Grossenbacher, a local artist who had a vision of creating a space where artists could exhibit and sell their work. Kathryn had always been passionate about art, and she had long dreamed of opening her own gallery. She worked hard to make her vision a reality, and after years of planning and preparation, Kathryn's Art Gallery finally opened its doors to the public.

At first, the gallery was a small operation, featuring only a few local artists. But as word spread about the quality of the art on display and the welcoming atmosphere of the gallery, it began to attract a wider audience. Kathryn was dedicated to supporting local artists, and she worked hard to create a space where they could showcase their work and connect with art enthusiasts in the community.

Over the years, Kathryn's Art Gallery has grown and evolved, but it has remained true to its original mission of supporting local artists. Today, the gallery features a diverse collection of art, including paintings, sculptures, and mixed media pieces. It is a beloved fixture in the local arts community, and it has helped to put the town on the map as a destination for art lovers.

One of the keys to the gallery's success has been Kathryn's unwavering commitment to the artists and the community. She has worked tirelessly to create a space that is welcoming and supportive, and she has been a mentor and friend to many of the artists who have exhibited there over the years. Thanks to Kathryn's passion and dedication, Kathryn's Art Gallery has become a vital part of the local arts scene, and it continues to inspire and delight visitors from near and far.
